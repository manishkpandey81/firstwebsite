Who are CCoE?

CCoE, expands to Cloud Center of Excellence. The team within Talent Cloud Services (TcCS) competency, consists of Architects with multiple skills. Team works on the new projects and ensures that we deliver what our clients expect from Talent. Team works on standard business hours and is based out of Isreal, India and Canada.

Team focuses on automation and project stability. Speaking of automation, the team analyzes the project enviroment and suggests the ways that can help improve the client experience and achieve a delightful experience as they do business with us.

This is not the Business As Usual (BAU) team that works on day to day in managing the client's environment. We have separate dedicated team that works on this and has dedicated focals who manage the day-in and day-out activities within the project. CCoE team works with these focals on the existing project and ensures that the project is stable and implements the new technologies, as and when appliable.
